Our Solutions

Comprehensive Cold Chain Solutions Tailored To Your Needs

From specialized pharmaceutical transport to high-volume food distribution, NiBha Logistics delivers end-to-end cold chain solutions designed for safety, reliability, and efficiency.

Different businesses have different cold chain needs. A pharmaceutical company requires strict temperature compliance and documentation. A dairy brand needs consistent chilled transport with quick turnaround. An ice cream distributor demands deep-frozen capability and volume handling. A poultry farm needs gentle transport for delicate hatching eggs.

At NiBha Logistics, we don't offer generic solutions — we provide specialized services tailored to your specific industry, cargo type, and business requirements. Our comprehensive portfolio covers every aspect of temperature-controlled logistics, backed by the expertise, infrastructure, and commitment to deliver excellence.

01

Precision Temperature Control From Origin To Destination

Temperature control isn't just about keeping things cold — it's about maintaining specific temperature ranges throughout the entire journey, regardless of external conditions, transit duration, or handling requirements. Our temperature-controlled transportation solution provides this precision through advanced equipment, systematic monitoring, and trained personnel.

1

Multi-Temperature Capability

Our fleet can maintain temperatures ranging from −25°C for deep-frozen products to +25°C for temperature-sensitive items requiring controlled ambient conditions.

2

Advanced Carrier AC Systems

Industry-leading Carrier air conditioning units with a pan-India service network maintain consistent temperatures even in India's extreme climates.

3

Insulated Container Design

High-quality insulation maintains temperature for 3–4 hours even without power, protecting cargo during loading, brief stops, or unexpected situations.

4

Real-Time Temperature Monitoring

Advanced sensors continuously monitor and log temperature throughout the journey, triggering immediate alerts for any deviation.

5

Temperature Validation

Pre-cooling procedures ensure vehicles reach required temperatures before loading; post-delivery documentation includes complete temperature logs.

6

Customized Cold Chain Solutions

Dedicated fleet options, specialized packaging, custom scheduling, and flexible contract terms tailored to your operational requirements.

02

Specialized Handling For Frozen And Chilled Products

Frozen and chilled products each have unique requirements for handling, loading, and transport. Our specialized protocols ensure products maintain quality, safety, and freshness throughout the journey, whether you're moving frozen meat at −18°C or chilled dairy at +4°C.

Frozen Product Handling (−18°C And Below)

1

Deep-Freeze Capability

Our refrigeration systems maintain −25°C and below, suitable for frozen foods, ice cream, frozen seafood, and frozen meat.

2

Rapid Loading Protocols

Trained teams execute quick loading procedures that minimize temperature exposure using pre-cooled containers and efficient workflows.

3

Volume Capacity

Our 32ft vehicles transport up to 960 ice cream crates, with various vehicle sizes accommodating different volume requirements.

Chilled Product Handling (0°C To +8°C)

1

Precision Chilling

Stable temperatures that prevent freezing while keeping products properly chilled within a narrow temperature band.

2

Hygiene Standards

Regular sanitization, food-grade interior finishes, and dedicated cleaning protocols appropriate for chilled food products.

3

First-In-First-Out Management

Coordinated loading sequences for multi-drop deliveries that enable efficient FIFO management and optimal freshness.

4

Temperature Transition Management

Careful management of transitions between temperature zones to prevent condensation, temperature shock, or product compromise.

Frozen Foods & Ready Meals Ice Cream & Frozen Desserts Frozen Meat, Poultry & Seafood Chilled Dairy Products Fresh Meat & Seafood Prepared Foods & Deli Items Temperature-Sensitive Bakery

05

Engineered For Fragile, Fertilized Cargo

Our hatching egg logistics service is engineered specifically for the safe movement of fragile fertilized eggs. Vehicles are equipped with vibration-dampening suspension systems and maintain precise temperature control between 18–20°C. Our trained drivers follow gentle handling protocols, ensuring minimal shock and stress to the eggs. With a dedicated fleet capable of transporting up to 750 boxes per vehicle, we provide reliable, specialized support for hatcheries and breeders.
