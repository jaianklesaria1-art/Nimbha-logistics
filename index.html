Industries We Serve

Specialized Solutions Across Diverse Industries

Different industries have different needs. Our cold chain expertise spans multiple sectors, each with unique temperature requirements, handling protocols, and regulatory compliance standards. We don't offer one-size-fits-all solutions — we deliver specialized service tailored to your industry.

Pharmaceuticals

Temperature-controlled transport for medicines, vaccines, and healthcare products requiring strict compliance and documentation. GDP-certified handling protocols ensure integrity from warehouse to healthcare facility.

Dairy Products

Safe transportation of milk, cheese, yogurt, butter, and other dairy products with precise temperature maintenance, preserving freshness and extending shelf life while meeting food safety standards.

Meat & Seafood

Frozen and chilled meat, poultry, and seafood logistics with hygiene-focused handling — from processing facilities to retail outlets, preserving quality, taste, and nutritional value.

Hatching Eggs

Specialized transport for fertilized eggs requiring gentle handling and precise temperature control. Drivers are trained in egg-specific protocols with vehicles equipped for delicate cargo.

Ice Cream

Deep-frozen transportation for ice cream products maintaining ultra-low temperatures. Our fleet capacity includes configurations that can transport up to 960 ice cream crates in our largest vehicles.

Chocolates & Confectionery

Temperature-sensitive chocolate transportation preventing melting and bloom. We understand the precise temperature windows required for different chocolate types and adjust our systems accordingly.

Fast Food & QSR

Reliable cold chain for quick-service restaurants and fast-food chains, supporting distribution networks with consistent delivery schedules and temperature compliance that meets franchise standards.

Agro & Floriculture

Fresh produce, flowers, and agricultural products requiring controlled temperature environments. From farm to market, we preserve freshness and extend product life through careful management.

About NiBha

Moving India's Cold Chain Forward

Bhanu Pratap Gurjar & Nitin Ravindra Singh are experienced professionals with deep expertise in cold chain logistics and supply chain management.

NiBha Logistics Private Limited provides seamless cold chain transportation across India with competitive pricing and minimal turnaround time. Founded on the principle that temperature-sensitive cargo deserves specialized attention, we've built our reputation on reliability, safety, and precision.

Our directors bring more than a decade of individual experience in logistics and supply chain management, combining to create a leadership team with deep industry knowledge and operational excellence. With a nationwide presence and professionally trained teams, we ensure safe and controlled transport for both chilled and frozen products across diverse industries.

Whether you're moving pharmaceutical products that demand strict temperature control, delicate hatching eggs requiring gentle handling, or frozen foods that must maintain quality throughout transit, NiBha Logistics delivers with consistency and care.

Our Solutions

Cold Chain Logistics Built For Your Business

From frozen to chilled to highly sensitive cargo, NiBha Logistics provides end-to-end cold chain solutions designed for safe and timely movement across India. Our comprehensive service portfolio addresses every aspect of temperature-controlled logistics, backed by technology, expertise, and commitment to excellence.

01

Temperature Controlled Transportation

Multi-temperature zone capability from −25°C to +25°C with advanced Carrier AC units, real-time monitoring, automated deviation alerts, and insulated containers holding safe conditions for 3–4 hours without power.

02

Frozen & Chilled Product Handling

Specialized protocols for frozen products (−18°C and below) and chilled items (0°C to +8°C), with strict pre-cooling, careful temperature transitions, and hygiene-controlled environments.

03

Multi-City Nationwide Distribution

A hub-and-spoke network connecting 27+ cities with intelligent routing that reduces transit time, supports multi-stop deliveries, and integrates with regional warehouses.

04

Real-Time Tracking & Monitoring

Live GPS tracking, temperature data logging, and automated deviation alerts — with a dedicated customer portal and mobile app integration for full shipment visibility.

05

Specialized Hatching Egg Logistics

Vibration-dampening suspension and precise 18–20°C control, with trained drivers following gentle handling protocols across a dedicated fleet of up to 750 boxes per vehicle.

06

Customized Cold Chain Solutions

Dedicated fleet options, specialized packaging, custom scheduling and routing, complete documentation compliance, and flexible contract terms tailored to your operations.
