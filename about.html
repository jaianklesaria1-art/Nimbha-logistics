Who We Are

Founded On Expertise, Infrastructure & Integrity

NiBha Logistics Private Limited is dedicated to providing reliable, efficient, and safe cold chain transport services across India. Founded on the principle that temperature-sensitive cargo deserves specialized attention and care, we've built our business on three pillars: expertise, infrastructure, and integrity.

Since our establishment in 2019, we've grown from a vision to a reality — expanding our presence across 27+ cities, building a modern fleet of temperature-controlled vehicles, and earning the trust of leading brands across pharmaceuticals, food, dairy, and other industries that depend on uncompromised cold chain integrity.

What sets us apart is our leadership. Our directors bring more than ten years of individual experience in logistics and supply chain management, combining to create a leadership team with 14+ years of collective expertise. This depth of knowledge informs every decision we make, from route optimization to technology investments to customer service protocols.

We understand that in cold chain logistics, there's no room for compromise. A broken cold chain doesn't just mean lost product — it means lost trust, lost business, and potentially serious safety implications. That's why we've built our entire operation around reliability, with multiple backup systems, trained personnel, and transparent communication at every stage.

Today, NiBha Logistics serves diverse industries with specialized needs — from the delicate nature of hatching egg transport to the strict temperature requirements of pharmaceutical products, from the volume demands of ice cream distribution to the freshness imperatives of dairy products. We handle it all with the same level of precision and care.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2000

Foundation Of Experience

Our directors begin building extensive experience in logistics and transportation, working across various sectors and developing deep expertise in supply chain operations. These formative years established the knowledge base that would later become NiBha Logistics.

2019

NiBha Logistics Established

Drawing on combined expertise and recognizing the growing need for specialized cold chain services, our directors establish NiBha Logistics Private Limited. The vision is clear: create a cold chain logistics company that prioritizes reliability, safety, and customer partnership.

2020

Building Infrastructure

Despite pandemic challenges, NiBha Logistics invests in building a modern fleet of temperature-controlled vehicles. Initial operations focus on major metropolitan markets, establishing reputation through consistent performance and customer-focused service.

2021

Network Expansion

Expanding beyond initial markets, NiBha Logistics establishes presence in additional cities across India. Strategic partnerships with major brands begin, validating the company's approach and creating opportunities for growth.

2022

Service Diversification

As client needs evolve, NiBha Logistics expands service offerings to cover diverse industries — from pharmaceuticals to ice cream, from dairy to hatching eggs. Specialized handling protocols are developed for each industry segment.

2023

Technology Integration

Real-time tracking systems, temperature monitoring technology, and customer portals are implemented, providing unprecedented visibility and control. The company crosses significant milestones in cities served and client partnerships.

2024

Strengthening Presence

Continued expansion across India, with presence now spanning 27+ major cities. Fleet modernization continues, and the company establishes itself as a trusted partner for cold chain logistics nationwide.

2025+

Vision For The Future

With a strong foundation built, NiBha Logistics focuses on becoming a truly pan-India cold chain leader. Plans include further network expansion, enhanced technology capabilities, sustainability initiatives, and deeper partnerships with clients across all industries served.
